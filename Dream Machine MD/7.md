Marble by WorldLabs
Marble is the first product from World Labs, a spatial intelligence
company that building frontier models that can perceive, generate, and
interact with the 3D world. It lets anyone create high-fidelity, persistent
3D worlds from just a single image, video, text prompt, or 3D layout.
From reality to fantasy.
Marble also lets you interactively edit, expand, and combine worlds.
Once generated, 3D worlds can be exported as Gaussian splats,
meshes, or videos. These new capabilities let users create and edit
worlds with fine-grained control; and makes those worlds more useful
than ever before.
Worldlabs have also launched [Marble Labs](https://www.worldlabs.ai/labs) , a creative hub where
imagination meets experimentation. For artists, engineers, and designers
who want to push the boundaries of world models, showcasing bold
ideas, real-world workflows, and new possibilities across gaming, VFX,
design, robotics, architecture, virtual production and beyond.
[DreamLab](https://www.linkedin.com/company/thedreamlabuk/) have been part of the beta testing for this over the last few
