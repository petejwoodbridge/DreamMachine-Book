The Coordination Collapse: Why AI is Rewriting the
Organizational Chart
The latest deep dive episode of the [Dream Machine podcast](https://open.spotify.com/show/2ptbLwVWeyO7ooPGHoYTqk) unpacks a
massive 120-page research report by [Dr John O'Hare](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jjohare/) - exploring a
monumental shift in how work is organised.
For two thousand years, organisational hierarchy has functioned not
merely as a management philosophy, but as an information routing
protocol built around the strict limits of human cognitive bandwidth .
Now, as artificial intelligence fundamentally removes these historical
bandwidth constraints, the traditional structural logic of the corporate
chart is actively dissolving.
The episode breaks down how this coordination collapse is not a future
prediction, but a present reality transforming the creative industries and
beyond. Listeners will discover that this transformation is already
occurring largely out of managements view, driven by the rise of
shadow AI.
